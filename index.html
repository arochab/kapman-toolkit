Workflow cards

Routes

Low End Ownership Kick, bass, mono, subtraction
  1. Solo kick and bass only.
  2. Decide who owns 40–90 Hz.
  3. Center lows first, widen later.
Stop rule

Stop when both read clearly at low volume.

Lead Focus Center, width, motion
  1. Choose one center voice.
  2. Add width from support, not from everything.
  3. Automate one motion source only.
Stop rule

Stop when the hook survives in mono.

Groove Lift Contrast, pocket, subtraction
  1. Mute one busy layer first.
  2. Push swing or nudge one percussive lane.
  3. Use automation for contrast, not clutter.
Stop rule

Stop when the loop moves without adding a new sound.

Finish Tonight Reference, bounce, leave it alone
  1. Run Mix Check once.
  2. Apply two fixes max.
  3. Compare against one reference and export.
Stop rule

Stop after the second bounce. No third micro-fix pass.
